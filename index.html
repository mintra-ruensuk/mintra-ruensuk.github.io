Mintra Ruensuk


(มินตรา รื่นสุข)
I am a data and analytics professional at Siam Commercial Bank (SCB), where I am responsible for ensuring the proper design, performance, and security of the analytics platform. I received my PhD in Creative Design Engineering from the Ulsan National Institute of Science and Technology (UNIST), where I was advised by Ian Oakley at the Interactions Lab and Hwajung Hong at the DxD Lab. My research interests include affective computing and human-computer interaction. In my work, I have focused on using machine learning for advanced data analysis and predictive modeling, and I have a strong background in software engineering and agile development methodologies. I have also taught at the undergraduate and graduate levels, providing mentorship and guidance to students in their academic and professional development.
