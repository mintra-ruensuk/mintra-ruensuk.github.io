Mintra Ruensuk


(มินตรา รื่นสุข)
I am a PhD Student in the Graduate School of Creative Design Engineering at Ulsan National Institute of Science and Technology (UNIST) - pronouced as "u-nist". I am advised by Ian Oakley at the Interactions Lab and Hwajung Hong at the DxD Lab. My research interests include affective computing and human-computer interaction.
